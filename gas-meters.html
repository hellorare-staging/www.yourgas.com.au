If you suspect a gas leak, eliminate ignition sources, move away from the area and call ATCO Gas on 13 13 52
Local call fee from anywhere in the state excluding mobiles.

Know where your gas meter is, in case you ever need it.

Knowing how to turn your gas meter on and off is important. If you feel a gas appliance is leaking gas it’s a good idea to switch off your gas meter and have the appliance checked.

Your gas meter is located in a metal meter box at the front of your property. It may be a gas-only meter box or a combined gas/electric meter box.
