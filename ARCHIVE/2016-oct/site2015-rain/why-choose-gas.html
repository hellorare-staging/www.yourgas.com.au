Why choose gas?

Used properly, natural gas is a safe, cost-effective and efficient
source of energy.

It is easy to use gas safely and securely at home if you maintain and take regular
care of your appliances and connections.

Here are a few of the reasons why gas is good for you – and the environment:

Savings

Natural gas appliances can be cheaper to run on an annual basis compared to electric appliances.

Switching to natural gas for heating, cooking and hot water could save you over $700 every year*. Rebates may be available for new connections to natural gas in select areas, and regardless of location there is no fee from ATCO Gas to connect your home to natural gas if works are carried out within 20 metres of the mains pipe.

Efficiency, versatility & convenience

Continuous hot water, instant warmth and controlled cooking in the kitchen and by converting your BBQ from LPG to natural gas are just some of the things that are possible with natural gas.

Natural gas can also dry your clothes, power your air conditioner in summer, even power your vehicle and produce electricity.

Natural gas can enhance the appeal and value of your property…

Natural gas has been shown to enhance the value of your property, meaning that when you want to sell, potential buyers may choose your house first.

Best of all, the network is underground which means there are no outages caused by…

Natural gas is recognised as one of the most efficient and eco-friendly sources of energy available.

The natural gas delivered by ATCO Gas comes from Western Australia’s abundant natural gas reserves. We are able to extract the gas and deliver it your home with fewer CO2 emissions than electricity produced from coal fired power stations.

Best of all, the network is underground which means there’s no outages caused by storms and no unsightly power towers crossing the landscape.

Leaks are rare, but the distinctive rotten egg odour added to natural gas allows for quick and easy detection in case of damage or faults.
